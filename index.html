Who We Are

Built by the People,
For the Planet

Terra Force is more than an organization — it's a community-rooted movement born from the shores of the Jaffna Peninsula. We believe that lasting environmental change starts at the grassroots level, with the people who call this land home.

Our work spans coastal cleanups that restore our lagoons and shores, circular economy initiatives that reimagine waste, and community forums that empower every citizen to become a guardian of nature.

What We Do

Our Initiatives

Flagship Program

Coastal & Lagoon Cleanups

Organized community-driven cleanup campaigns along the Jaffna Peninsula's coastline and lagoon systems, removing plastic waste and restoring natural habitats.

CEF 1.0

Circular Economy Forum

Introducing circular economy principles to the Northern Region — bringing together community leaders, entrepreneurs, and youth to reimagine how we produce, consume, and recycle.

Awareness

Community Education

Workshops, forums, and grassroots campaigns that empower every citizen — from school children to village elders — to become stewards of their environment.
